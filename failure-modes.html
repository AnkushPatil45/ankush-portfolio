FAILURE MODE ANALYSIS

A study of cloud infrastructure resilience and security detection gaps

AUTHOR: Ankush Patil
SCOPE: AWS / AZURE / EDR TELEMETRY
DATE: 2026-02-24
01_ANALYSIS_MATRIX
VEC_EDR_01 CRITICAL

Sensor Blind Spots & Tampering

Analysis of endpoint detection gaps where EDR sensors (CrowdStrike/SentinelOne) are uninstalled or services are silenced via administrative compromise. Investigation focuses on detection latency and alerting failures during unmanaged asset execution.

VEC_IAM_04 CRITICAL

Cloud IAM Privilege Escalation

Mapping misconfigured IAM policies in AWS/Azure that allow users to update their own permissions or assume high-privileged roles. Focus on 'Resource Star' policies and cross-account trust relationship vulnerabilities.

VEC_NET_07 ELEVATED

Firewall Shadow Rules

Audit of over-permissive security group rules that "shadow" stricter policies, leading to unintended port exposure (RDP/SSH) across VPC peering connections.

VEC_OBS_02 MODERATE

Observability Cost Explosion

Research into high-cardinality log data causing runaway costs in SIEM (Splunk/Sentinel). Developing filtering strategies to maintain security visibility while optimizing ingest volume.

02_APPLICATION_RESILIENCE

PROJECT_CASE: HOUSE OF NORTH

Implementation of an e-commerce infrastructure with a focus on mitigating single points of failure and unauthorized access vectors.

INFRA_RELIABILITY

Implemented multi-AZ deployment strategies to ensure platform availability during regional service disruptions.

SECURE_REUSABILITY

Developed modular Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) templates with embedded security guardrails to prevent misconfigurations.

IDENTITY_HYGIENE

Enforced Least Privilege Principle across all service accounts, reducing the blast radius of potential IAM compromises.

03_REMEDIATION_PROTOCOLS

Architectural Hardening

Standardizing on 'Default-Deny' security group configurations and implementing automated drift detection to catch shadow rules before they reach production environments.

Observability Discipline

Developing custom telemetry filters at the ingest layer to prioritize high-fidelity security events, significantly reducing SIEM costs while maintaining EDR coverage.
